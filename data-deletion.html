Data Deletion Request

If you wish to request the deletion of your personal data from our system, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your registered email address and any relevant account details in the email.
  3. We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
